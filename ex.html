ANTI NEW NORM



This organization serves as a catalyst for teenagers and young adults to address and rectify their mistakes. It not only provides a platform for individuals to express the challenges they encounter but also fosters empowerment through innovative strategies and captivating activities designed to channel their potential and creativity.

OUR PURPOSE

By involving young individuals in both community and volunteer services, we impart the significance of contributing to society.

This endeavor fosters a deep connection with the well-being of others.


Our emphasis lies in empowering youth to articulate their viewpoints, champion their rights, and actively drive positive transformations.

OUR MISSION

  • Saving children
  • Peace upon communities/societies
  • Care and Protection

OUR VISION

  • Community impact
  • Long term perspective
  • Inspiration and Direction

OUR SERVICES

  • Community service and volunteering
  • Counseling and support services
  • Recreational activities
  • Peer support groups
